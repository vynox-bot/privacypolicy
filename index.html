Vynox Bot – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 25th of July 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Vynox (“the Bot”) collects, uses, and protects your data when you use its features.

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic identifiers such as user IDs, server IDs, and channel IDs.
  • Command-related message content, only when needed for the Bot to operate.
  • Configuration or settings data necessary for custom features (e.g., server preferences).

2. How We Use the Data

  • To provide and improve the Bot’s features.
  • To ensure proper functionality of commands and server settings.
  • To maintain stability and prevent abuse of the Bot’s services.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share or sell your data to third parties. Data will only be disclosed if required by law or to enforce these Terms.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Only minimal data required for the Bot’s operation is stored.

  • Temporary command-related data is deleted automatically after use.
